Built VECTOR, a full-stack real-time movement tracking platform in Next.js 15 with Prisma + SQLite. The app tracks global air traffic, NYC Citi Bike stations, and MTA subway lines across 7 pages with a tactical dark UI aesthetic inspired by NASA cockpits. Implemented 12 isolated components with 41 component scenarios, 16 app-level scenarios, complete design token system (zero hardcoded values), TDD lib functions with 98 tests, and interactive Leaflet maps with live aircraft and bike station overlays.
